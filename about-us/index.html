About us

Located in St. Paul Minnesota, Avenues Recovery is an outpatient and co-occurring disorders clinic. We are here to support you wherever you are in your recovery journey.

We aim to provide individualized treatment for adults who experience problematic substance use and co-occurring mental health issues. Our goal is to address the health and well-being of our clients to improve overall wellness - Julie Hooker, Treatment Director, MS LADC

Who we are

Our treatment philosophy is based on client centered and trauma-informed care. We meet each client where they are, offering support to achieve their treatment goals with respect to culture, language and orientation. We believe in doing more than treating a diagnosis - we treat people, each with unique needs, experiences, and belief systems.

Julie Hooker | Treatment Director, MS, LADC

Julie brings with her years of experience in the field of substance use and recovery. She received her LADC through Minneapolis Community and Technical College and went on to obtain a Bachelor's degree in Human Services at Metropolitan State University. She earned her Master's degree in Social and Community Services at Capella University. Julie is a native Minnesotan and understands the issues facing our homeless and substance using populations. As the owner and director of Avenues Recovery, Julie brings this wealth of knowledge and experience to implement a client-centered and trauma-informed approach, focusing on substance use and mental health issues. By utilizing best practices and a collaborative relationship with the clients, Julie hopes to promote wellness through a continuum of care model that supports the stages of change and creates a positive relationship with the client throughout their journey of recovery.
